The Story

Three arcs in one operator.

PM craft, AI-driven CRO, and founder mileage — sharpened against real P&L.

I'm a Senior Product Leader with 10+ years of building products that move numbers. I cut my teeth on eCommerce and SaaS, and I've spent the last several years doubling down on AI — not as a buzzword, but as the substrate for search, personalization, agentic automation, and CRO. The headline outcome: $21M+ in measurable business impact for a $100M-ARR US-based company, delivered by combining product strategy with applied AI.

Before that, I went 0→1 twice. I co-founded Viazene and SuperParent — both secured seed funding and VC incubation. Earlier, at Vrozart, my work on technology-led innovation won the Indian Achiever's Award (2021). Founder mode taught me to ship under constraint, to treat the roadmap as a hypothesis, and to never confuse activity with outcome.

Today I split time between shipping AI tooling for daily PM workflows (jira-digest, intraday trading agents, AI-assisted hiring flows, an interactive Google Flow vision concept) and helping teams turn AI from a demo into a durable line item. I'm based in Mumbai, currently on-site in Bengaluru, and open to remote/hybrid roles — with a 15-day notice period.
